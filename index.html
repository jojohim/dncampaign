About Us

We are committed to conserve and protect the natural environment in Denmark in order to secure a tomorrow, where natural forests and meadows rich in biological diversity still exists and clean drinking water is still obtainable.

We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience and enjoy nature. Therefore, we encourage children and adults to use nature more.
